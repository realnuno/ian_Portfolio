Travelers

HTML5 | CSS3 | JavaScript ES6 | React | Node.js

"Travelers" is website that allows you to find your next travel destination and record personalized notes for vacation planning. When you search for any destination in the world, you will get recommendations for nice venues and places to visit in the area and detailed information about those places including pictures and maps. You can conveniently save your results along with any notes you've made in your personalized list.
